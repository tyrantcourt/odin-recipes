Lemon Pepper Salmon

Home

Description

Salmon with lemon and pepper. I like this quite a bit! If salmon were cheaper- and we went to the store on a more regular basis- I'd have this weekly.

Ingredients

  • Salmon Fillet
  • Lemon Juice
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Olive Oil

Prep Materials

  • Bowl to mix sauce in
  • Large cast iron pan to cook fillet in
  • Plate to hold fillet between seasoning and cooking

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Melt butter in sauce bowl, whisk in lemon juice, salt and pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Set each fillet into sauce to soak, then move to plate.
  4. Heat olive oil in pan on stovetop, then cook fillets in it for about 1 minute, to sear the outside.
  5. Move the pan into the preheated oven to cook until the salmon flakes easily with a fork, about 10 to 12 minutes.
